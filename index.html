Welcome to Meraki Lifecare

Meraki Lifecare is a registered NDIS provider. We provide disability support, aged care services and post acute nursing care.

We have an experienced team of registered nurses and disability support workers. Our management team includes well trained disability support staff and nurses with over 25 years of experience in various roles within the health care industry.

We work in close partnership with both you and your family, to help you achieve maximum independence and focus on your abilities.

At Meraki, we believe that community access, inclusion and equality are fundamental rights of every individual. We enable you to recognise and accommodate for your personal needs and preferences. Our care is specially designed to improve the physical, emotional and social wellbeing of the people we care for.

Meraki brings convenient and professional nursing services to your home. Our specialised team is dedicated to delivering quality care and support. In-home nursing care provides attentive medical and domestic support to achieve your optimal health and wellbeing.
