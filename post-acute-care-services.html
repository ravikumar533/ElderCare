  • Nursing Care - Post Acute

    Receive clinical support while you recover from an illness or injury

We offer post acute clinical care at home. Our Nurses will come to your place and work closely with your hospital team for your care and support. Our post acute aims to: ● Support for healing and recovery ● Reduce the risk of readmission in the hospital ● Provide timely responds to the medical needs ● Provide a flexible services that responds to each clients particular needs, Our clinical Nursing care includes ● Assistance with medication. ● Management of your continence ● Changing wound dressing ● Checking blood pressure ● Assistance with travelling to and from medical appointments. ● Organising visits from allied healthcare care professionals ● Assistance with other clinical needs advised by your doctor.
